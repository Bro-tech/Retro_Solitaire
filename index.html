HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE: Move all cards to foundation piles by suit from Ace to King.

TABLEAU: Build down in alternating colors (red on black, black on red).

FOUNDATIONS: Build up by suit starting with Aces.

STOCK: Draw cards from the deck when no moves are available.

WINNING: Complete all four foundation piles to win!
